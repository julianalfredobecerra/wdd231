Member Spotlights

Brightline Marketing Solutions

Industry: Digital Marketing & Advertising
Highlight: Brightline Marketing has helped over 200 local businesses grow their online presence with custom strategies in SEO, social media, and content creation. Recently named one of the "Top 10 Marketing Agencies in the Region," Brightline is committed to helping small businesses shine in a digital world.
Fun Fact: They offer free digital audits to Chamber members each quarter!

Green Horizon Solar

Industry: Renewable Energy & Sustainability
Highlight: A leader in sustainable energy solutions, Green Horizon Solar recently completed the city's largest residential solar project. Their commitment to clean energy and community education makes them a shining example of innovation and impact.
Chamber Connection: Green Horizon regularly sponsors the Chamber’s Green Business Awards and hosts free energy workshops.

Cornerstone Coffee Co.

Industry: Food & Beverage / Hospitality
Highlight: This family-owned café has become a local favorite for its community-first vibe, artisan brews, and support of local artists. Cornerstone recently expanded to a second location and continues to collaborate with neighborhood schools and nonprofits.
Member Perk: Chamber members receive 15% off catering and meeting space rentals.
